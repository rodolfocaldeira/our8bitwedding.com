02. How to get to Quinta da Taipa?

Quinta da Taipa is in Porto da Luz, Alenquer, about 30min from Lisbon.

  1. Alenquer / Carregado Exit
  2. Follow signs Alenquer
  3. Go around the round/about toward Alenquer
  4. After crossing the river, follow stright ahead (400m), turn left heading to Torres Vedras
  5. Straight ahead until you pass by Porto da Luz

GPS: N 39º3'57" | W 9º1'58"
